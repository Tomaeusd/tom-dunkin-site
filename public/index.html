Press

"After the successes of indie games such as Battle Chef Brigade and Celeste in perfectly capturing the tone of their story through their music, the bar for indie game soundtracks has risen."
- Jordon Ramee, OnlySP

"Anime characters are superimposed on soft backgrounds featuring wet washes of paint pooled over textured paper, set to a lilting orchestral soundtrack not unlike a Miyazaki score."
- Holly Green, Paste Magazine

"And I love so, so much of its execution, especially the gorgeous art and music."
- Helen Rosner, Polygon

"Music, art, and voice acting are all top notch, giving it a high-quality JRPG feel throughout."
- Chris Brown, Gameplanet

"The soundtrack is also really quite charming and serves as a nice cherry on top."
- Nat Eker, Push Square

"The gorgeous water colour visuals, charming characters, local multiplayer component, and fantastic soundtrack definitely make this a must-have indie gem."
- A.J. Maciejewski, Video Chums

"Battle Chef Brigade‘s art style is beautiful. Its hand-drawn 2D style mixes perfectly with the relaxing and peaceful music."
- Stan Cohen, GameSpew

"Couple that voice acting with some superb music, and 'now you’re cooking with gas.' There wasn’t an excessive amount of music for the game (much of the tracks were reused for matches and chores), but the music and sound effects were used perfectly to accentuate the mood of whatever was going on. I got major 90’s/00’s anime vibes, which tweaked my nostalgia as I played. It’s definitely a soundtrack that’s worth listening to."
- Rutledge Daugette, TechRaptor
